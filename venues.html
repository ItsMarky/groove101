The Premier Party Band for London, Kent & The South East

Groove 101 is an award-winning party band delivering unforgettable live music for events across London, Kent, and the entire South East of England. With over a decade of experience, we are the trusted choice for weddings, corporate functions, and private parties.

Corporate Events & Private Parties in London

We have extensive experience performing across London, with a special focus on the vibrant Royal Borough of Greenwich. Our work here includes providing entertainment for prestigious events, from historic locations like Severndroog Castle to the Civic Awards, where we were honoured for our contributions to local arts and entertainment.

Your Perfect Wedding & Function Band in Kent

As a Kent-based band, we have a special love for performing across the 'Garden of England.' Featured on BBC Radio Kent, we are the perfect fit for the county's stunning venues. We regularly perform in and around Tunbridge Wells, Tonbridge, and Sevenoaks, and are the go-to choice for events in major towns like Maidstone and the historic city of Canterbury. Whether it's a wedding at Penshurst Place or a party in Ashford, our goal is always to get everyone on the dance floor.

Available Across London & The South East

Based in the heart of Kent, we are perfectly positioned to bring the party to events across London and the entire South East. We have extensive experience performing in Sussex, Surrey, and beyond, and we love travelling to new venues. To ensure the energy never drops, our performance includes curated party music played between our live sets at no extra cost, guaranteeing a seamless and unforgettable night of entertainment.
